Think Cultural Health

Newsletter April 2023

This quarterly Newsletter highlights CLAS in Action. CLAS stands for Culturally and Linguistically Appropriate Services. CLAS is a Way to provide respectful and responsive services, improve quality of care, and advance health equity. To guide organizations in providing CLAS, OMH developed a set of 15 action Steps called the National CLAS Standards.
